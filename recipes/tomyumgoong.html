Tom Yum Goong

Description

Tom yum (ต้มยำ) is a type of Thai hot and sour soup where the iconic
flavours come from 3 herbs: lemongrass, galangal, and makrut (kaffir)
lime leaves. It can be made with any meat or seafood, even leftover cooked meats,
but the most popular version is made with shrimp, i.e. tom yum goong ต้มยำกุ้ง
(goong means shrimp).
