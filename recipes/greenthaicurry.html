Green Thai Curry

Description

Green curry is the most popular and one of the most well-known dishes in Thai cuisine. If you want a completely authentic recipe, and one that maximizes deliciousness, this is it right here. It is one of the best known dishes of Thai cuisine, and despite its complex flavours, it is actually quite easy to make at home. It's also gluten free, and adaptable for vegans!

Ingredients

Here are ingredients you'll need for an authentic Thai green curry. The bamboo shoots are a common vegetable used for green curry, but feel free to change it up. And if you are DIY-minded and have time, here's my homemade green curry paste recipe.

  • 1 cup Thai Basil
  • 1½ cup Bamboo Shoots, canned (can use sliced or strips)
  • 1 spur chillies or ¼ red bell pepper, julienned
  • 50g Green curry paste, store bought or homemade
  • 3-4 Makrut lime leaves, roughly torn
  • 1¾ Full fat coconut milk, divided
  • 1½ - 2 Tbsp Fish sauce
  • 2Tbsp Palm sugar, finely chopped (sub granulated or light brown sugar)
  • 1lb Boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup Chicken stock, unsalted
  • Jasmine rice for serving
